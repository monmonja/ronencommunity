Baxie Wiki

Where to start?

  1. To get started, ensure you have a ronin wallet.
  2. There are 2 ways to get baxie:
    1. Get an egg and hatch it (see Have an egg?)
    2. Get a baxie from ronin marketplace or openseas. (See Where to get baxie?)
  3. While waiting for the game you can earn RP and $RONEN by logging in daily. (See Login Requirements)

Where to get baxie?

On ronin:
You can get them on Opensea.io or Ronin marketplace

On abstract:
You can get them on Opensea.io

Have an egg?

  1. Head over https://ronen.network/baxie
  2. Log in to your Ronen.Network account
  3. Choose the egg you want to hatch — then click Hatch

    You can Reroll Baxie Parts (Max 3 times; No time limit)
    1st reroll → 1,000 $RONEN
    2nd reroll → 2,000 $RONEN
    3rd reroll → 3,000 $RONEN

    Remember to lock the parts you dont want to reroll.

Login Requirements

  1. Be in Ronin network (Abstract is not yet available)
  2. Hold atleast 1 Baxie
  3. 3,000 $RONEN inside the RONEN website (see Ronen Wiki)
