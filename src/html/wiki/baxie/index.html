Your baxies

Once you have an egg. Head over https://ronen.network/baxie.
Log in to your Ronen.Network account, head to Baxie, and choose the egg you want to hatch — then click Hatch.
You can Reroll Baxie Parts (Max 3 times; No time limit)
1st reroll → 1,000 $RONEN
2nd reroll → 2,000 $RONEN
3rd reroll → 3,000 $RONEN

🎭 Rarity
Rarity is based on the number of Pure/Mystic parts your Baxie has.

⚔️ Stats
Each Baxie comes with Attack, Stamina, and Defense:
Attack → Base damage
Stamina → Number of skill uses
Defense → Base defense

Base Stats by Pure/Mystic Parts:
1/6 → 40–49
2/6 → 50–59
3/6 → 60–69
4/6 → 70–79
5/6 → 80–89
6/6 → 90–99

🔹 Each Mystic Part adds +3 Attack, +3 Stamina, +3 Defense.

🌀 Skills
1–2 Pure/Mystic Parts → 1 Skill
3–4 Pure/Mystic Parts → 2 Skills
5-6 Pure/Mystic Parts → 3 Skills

📌 Phase 1: Fixed skills per class.
📌 Phase 2: Main skills per part will be introduced + free skill rerolls (based on your Phase 1 rerolls).

🧩 Class Advantages
⚔️ Attack +10 → Fire (explosive/aggressive), Demon (dark offensive)
🛡️ Defense +10 → Aqua (protective/adaptive), Plant (resilient)
💪 Stamina +10 → Electric (endurance), Fairy (sustained magical support)
