Where to start?

  1. To get started, ensure you have a ronin wallet.
  2. Next, get a plant from either the Ronin Marketplace or OpenSea.
    3. You'll need $pvr to purchase in-game items like small pot and water.
  3. You can obtain $pvr by either using Ronin Swap or Kyberswap. Swap a minimum of 4,000 $pvr.
    If your transaction fails, edit the gas limit to 20 million.
  4. Once you have both a plant and enough $pvr, go to the official site:
    Plant Vs Ronke.
  5. Click "Login with Ronin Wallet".
  6. Click on the wallet icon located in the bottom right corner.
  7. Then click Deposit and copy the address.
  8. In your Ronin wallet, locate and click on $pvr, click Send. Paste the address you copied earlier and click Next. Finally, Confirm the transaction.

  10. Click on the open pot (the first pot), then select the NFT you purchased.
  11. Click on the pot opened (first pot) and click on the nft you purchased.
  12. Go to the Shop (second icon from the left).
  13. Buy 1 Water and 1 Small Pot.
  14. Click Home, then click the pot icon on your plant.
  15. Then click the water icon and use the Water.
