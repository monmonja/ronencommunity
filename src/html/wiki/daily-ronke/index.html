Where to start?

  1. To get started, ensure you have a ronin wallet.
  2. Next, get a dronker from either the Opensea.io.
  3. You’ll need $dron to buy a Cave (500 $dron) and a Pickaxe (100 $dron).
  4. You can obtain $dron by either using Ronin Network or Kyberswap.
    If your transaction fails, edit the gas limit to 20 million.
  5. Once you have both a dronker and enough $dron, go to the official site:
    Daily Ronke.
  6. Click Connect Wallet (top-right) and connect your Ronin Wallet.
  7. Click the NFTs icon and select Deposit to Game on your Dronker.
  8. Agree to the terms, then click Confirm Deposit and approve the action in your wallet.
  9. Click the Wallet icon, scroll down to find your deposit address, then click Copy Address and confirm.
  10. In your Ronin wallet, locate and click on $dron, click Send. Paste the address you copied earlier and click Next. Finally, Confirm the transaction.
  11. Wait about 2–5 minutes for the transaction to reflect in your account, reload the page after waiting.
