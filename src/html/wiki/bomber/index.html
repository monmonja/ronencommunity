Where to start?

  1. To get started, ensure you have a ronin wallet.
  2. Next, get a common hero from either the Ronin marketplace or Opensea.io.
  3. To activate the hero, you'll need $bomb, at least 1,000 per hero (see Nft requirements).
  4. You can obtain $bomb by either using Ronin Network or Kyberswap.
    If your transaction fails, edit the gas limit to 20 million.
  5. Once you have both a common hero and enough $bomb, go to the official site:
    Bomber ronin.
  6. Click "Login with Ronin Wallet".
  7. Click on the wallet icon located in the bottom right corner.
  8. Then click Deposit and copy the address.
  9. In your Ronin wallet, locate and click on $bomb, click Send. Paste the address you copied earlier and click Next. Finally, Confirm the transaction.
