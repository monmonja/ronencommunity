Where to start?

  1. To get started, ensure you have a ronin wallet.
  2. Next, get a doll from either the Ronin marketplace or Opensea.io.
  3. You'll need $doll to activate the battlefield, where your dolls go to battle, each one requires 3,000 $doll. It's a good idea to get extra $doll to buy food, potions, weapons, and armor.
  4. You can obtain $doll by either using Ronin Network or Kyberswap.
    If your transaction fails, edit the gas limit to 20 million.
  5. Once you have both a doll and enough $doll, go to the official site:
    Doll Fantasy RPG.
  6. Click the Connect Wallet with your Ronin Wallet.
  7. Click on the piggy bank icon (bottom right icon).
  8. Click on deposit and copy address.
  9. In your Ronin wallet, locate and click on $doll, click Send. Paste the address you copied earlier and click Next. Finally, Confirm the transaction.
  10. Wait about 2â€“5 minutes for the transaction to reflect in your account, reload the page after waiting.
  11. Click on the shop icon, then purchase the following items:
    • 1 Steak
    • 1 Elixir
    • (Optional) Leather Armor
    • (Optional) Spear
  12. Click Home icon (bottom left icon), click on Doll Battle.
  13. Tap the big plus (+) icon at bottom of the tile and choose your doll.
  14. Click on the steak icon on top right color and select one steak.
  15. There are 4 small icons. Click each one to deploy the items you purchased from the in-game store.
