Where to start?

  1. To get started, ensure you have a ronin wallet.
  2. Next, get a basic miner from either the Ronin marketplace or Opensea.io.
  3. To activate the miner, you'll need $RONEN, at least 3,000 per basic miner (see Nft requirements).
  4. You can obtain $RONEN by either using Ronin Network or Kyberswap.
    If your transaction fails, edit the gas limit to 20 million.
  5. Once you have both a basic miner and enough $RONEN, go to the official site:
    ronen.network
  6. Click Connect Wallet, located on top right of the page.
  7. Then click Deposit and enter the amount of $RONEN you wish to deposit. Verify it with your ronin wallet.
  8. Wait about 2â€“5 minutes for the transaction to reflect in your account, reload the page after waiting.
  9. You can update your miners, which uses $RONEN, to increase your hashpower.
    To start upgrading, scroll down to where your miners are and click Upgrade.
    To know how much $RONEN per level, see upgrading a miner.
    If you reached your miner to level 15, it will have additional bonus, as indicated in Max Level Hash power Boost.
  10. You can also multiply your earnings by locking your $RONEN.
    To start your multiplier, click the LOCKED tab and then the Lock button.
    Enter the amount you want to lock, to see how much per multipliers, check out Multiplier Tiers.
