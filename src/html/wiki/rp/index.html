Where can i use RP?

Ronen Network GTDs of projects under RONEN, in pre-sales.
Baxie Game
  1. Breeding cooldown → reduced with RP
  2. Hatching time → reduced with RP
  3. RP will also be used for upcoming in-game items in BAXIE.

BAXIE holder

All you have to do is hold a baxie in your wallet. For each baxie refer to the table below.


Base/Body 50 RP
Per pure part 10 RP
Per mystic part 20 RP
Unhatched 50 RP
Important: All BAXIE listed on the marketplace will NOT generate RP. Only those being held (not listed) will earn RP.
