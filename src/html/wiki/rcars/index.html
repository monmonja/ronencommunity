Where to start?

  1. To get started, ensure you have a ronin wallet.
  2. Next, get a car from either the Ronin marketplace or Opensea.io.
  3. You'll need $rcar to purchase in-game items for racing. The minimum requirement is 250 $rcar for a fuel kit and 100 $rcar for a repair kit.
  4. You can obtain $rcar by either using Ronin Network or Kyberswap.
    If your transaction fails, edit the gas limit to 20 million.
  5. Once you have both a car and enough $rcar, go to the official site:
    Ronencars.tech
  6. Click the Connect Ronin Wallet from the main content (Not the top menu, it doesn't redirect to dashboard)
  7. Click on the menu (hamburger icon on the mobile) then click wallet, bottom right icon.
  8. Click copy address.
  9. In your Ronin wallet, locate and click on $rcar, click Send. Paste the address you copied earlier and click Next. Finally, Confirm the transaction.
  10. Wait about 2â€“5 minutes for the transaction to reflect in your account, reload the page after waiting.
  11. Click on the menu (hamburger icon on the mobile) then click Marketplace.
  12. Buy a fuel kit and confirm it by clicking Confirm Purchase.
  13. Click on the menu (hamburger icon on the mobile) then click Dashboard.
  14. Click on the fuel icon to fuel your car (top left icon).
  15. Click on the race icon (bottom right icon) and watch and enjoy your race.
